# AdSense (Google Display Network) Advertising Blueprint
Target Audiences: Thai, English (Expats), and Chinese

This guideline focuses exclusively on advertising your property across the **Google AdSense Network** (used by advertisers via the **Google Display Network** or GDN).
By running these campaigns, your highly visual banner ads will appear exclusively on millions of worldwide websites, news portals, blogs, and apps that monetize their traffic using Google AdSense.

## The Strategy: Actionable Banner Ads & Retargeting
Because users on AdSense partner sites are usually reading articles or browsing the web (not actively searching to buy a condo), Display Network banner ads must be **highly visual**, display the price clearly, and create a sense of urgency.

## Technical Setup Guidelines for AdSense Network Ads

1.  **Multiple Banner Sizes:** AdSense publishers use many different ad slots. You must create your graphic banners in these key dimensions to ensure they display across all partner websites:
    *   `300x250` (Medium Rectangle)
    *   `728x90` (Leaderboard - Top of websites)
    *   `160x600` (Wide Skyscraper - Sides of websites)
    *   `320x50` (Mobile Leaderboard)

2.  **Tracking Setup:**
    Ensure you use different tracking links (UTM parameters) when creating the banners for each language. This allows you to track exactly which AdSense banner resulted in a buyer messaging you on LINE:
    *   Thai Banner URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/?utm_source=adsense&utm_campaign=condo_thai`
    *   English Banner URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/?utm_source=adsense&utm_campaign=condo_english`
    *   Chinese Banner URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/?utm_source=adsense&utm_campaign=condo_chinese`

3.  **Retargeting Pixel:** Set up the Google Ads retargeting tag on your website's `index.html`. This ensures that anyone who visits your website—but leaves without scanning your LINE QR code—will automatically be shown your AdSense banner ads while they browse other sites over the next 30 days.
