This guideline is designed to help you strategically target three distinct buyer demographics for the dcondo Ping property. Since each demographic has different motivations and uses different platforms, we have segmented the ad copy and targeting strategies accordingly.

## 🚀 Recommended Campaign Launch Strategy

1.  **Pixel Setup:** Install the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics on your website to track clicks coming from the QR code and forms.
2.  **A/B Testing:** Run the Thai ad to Bangkok residents and the English ad to expats in Thailand simultaneously. Use a budget of ฿500/day for 7 days to see which demographic generates clicks at a lower cost (Cost Per Click).
3.  **Cross-Platform Retargeting:** People rarely buy on their first viewing. Once they view your website, you can use Google Display network to show them photos of `dcondo Ping` while they read the news online to remind them to contact you!
